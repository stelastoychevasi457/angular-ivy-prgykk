List of people from "SPEED GORILLA"

"SPEED GORILLA" is an all student (high school and university inclusive) event for newcomers to the technology give the fast typing a try.
Everyone can join from begginers to pros, the key is to have fun and show everyone what you are capable of. The event is always held a month after the school year starts. This way students can organize better and have support and company by their own school, friends and family.
Even if you get unlucky to be by yourself, do not fret, because we have a lot of other distractions and gaming events happening at once to help you out.
